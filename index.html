Dermal Filler

Hyaluronic Acid (HA) gel is a temporary and reversible dermal filler. These fillers are composed of natural sugar molecules found in our bodies. There are many different types of dermal fillers to choose from, it all depends on how much is required. At Doctor Aesthetics, we carry a large range of HA dermal fillers to help enhance facial contours of cheeks, nose, lips and jawline, instantly restore lost volume, hydrate, soften fine lines, smooth out dynamic lines, lift and contour the face with minimal downtime.
